About TipToe Soles

TipToe Soles is dedicated to helping customers find shoes that support their lifestyle, without sacrificing style or comfort.

Our Story

TipToe Soles started as a small local retailer with a simple goal: to make comfortable shoes easier to find. Many customers were experiencing foot pain from unsupportive footwear, so we focused on shoes with better cushioning and support.

Today, we offer a carefully selected range suitable for work, casual outings and everyday wear. We listen to feedback to improve our range and bring in new styles that meet real-world needs.

What We Offer

  • Comfortable and supportive footwear for everyday use
  • Styles suitable for both casual and formal occasions
  • Friendly, personalised service to help you find the right fit
  • A focus on long-lasting materials and quality construction

Our Focus on Comfort

We understand that every foot is different. Many of our styles offer features such as removable insoles, arch support and adjustable straps to help you customise your fit.

Our aim is to ensure you can walk confidently and comfortably throughout your day.

Why Shop With Us?

  • Monochromatic, modern store design that is easy to navigate
  • Helpful staff who understand foot comfort and support
  • Carefully chosen brands and models
  • Focus on both comfort and style
