Our Services

We offer a range of services to help you find the right footwear and keep your shoes comfortable for longer.

In-store Fitting & Support

  • Personalised fitting to help you choose the right size and width.
  • Advice on support features such as arch support and cushioning.
  • Guidance on suitable footwear for work, walking or casual wear.

Product Care Advice

  • Tips for cleaning and caring for your footwear.
  • Advice on how to extend the life of your shoes.
  • Recommendations for insoles and accessories.

Special Orders

  • Assistance with ordering sizes that are not currently in stock.
  • Support for specific comfort requirements.
  • Clear communication on expected delivery timeframes.
